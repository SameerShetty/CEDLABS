About

CEDLabs Pvt Ltd is emerging product designing, developing and manufacturing firm. CEDLabs started in 2017 with group of top engineers working in different sectors like Aerospace, Railways and Telecom. The Aim of the CEDLabs is to be electronic product designing and manufacturing company. CEDLabs started with designing and development of RFID handheld reader in 2018 and in span of Two years, product is fully functional and ready for Marketing. The Product UAM-2P-191 featured with an unique design to address the complexity issues in assembly process control, inventory management and provides an unique identification for any time to market product.

Services

Software

CEDLabs has in-house combination of Doctorate and Young Android and Web Server based Application developing engineers. UAM-2P-191 Android is Designed in CEDLabs and can be customizable as per customer needs.

Electronics

One of the director is specialized in Power Electronics and Digital HW. We have in-house team of HW Designing, development and testing Integration and Assembly We have a assembly area in Tumkur. Final assembly, testing and delivery will done from our Tumkur Factory facility.

Mechanical

UAM-2P-191 plastic mechanical design is developed in house and for all prototyping, a 3D printingtechnology was installed in office for all our trials. Product design is very much unique in nature and can say first in the market that can support multipurpose like under/up ground asset tracking.
